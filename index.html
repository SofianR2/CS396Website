To Dare is to Do

We are Tottenham Hotspur. Born from the spirit of North London in 1882, we carry the legacy of footballing excellence and unwavering determination. Our club represents more than just a team – we are a family united by passion, tradition, and the relentless pursuit of glory. From the hallowed grounds of White Hart Lane to our magnificent new home at Tottenham Hotspur Stadium, we have always played with style, flair, and the courage to dream big. Our motto "To Dare is to Do" isn't just words – it's our way of life, our commitment to attacking football, and our refusal to accept anything less than our best. We are the Lilywhites, the Spurs, and we carry the hopes and dreams of millions around the world. Together, we rise, we fight, and we dare to achieve the impossible.
